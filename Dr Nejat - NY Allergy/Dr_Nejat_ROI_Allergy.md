# MiniBox+ Return on Investment — NY Allergy and Sinus Centers

Prepared by Erik Bernard, PulmOne Advanced Medical Devices

The MiniBox+ is a one time purchase of **$37,800** (quote 00007445). Here is what it returns once it is running in your office.

## The per test economics

| | Per full panel |
|---|---|
| Reimbursement (94060 + 94726 + 94729) | ~$147 |
| Cost to run (mouthpiece + DLCO gas) | ~$5 |
| **Net before staff time** | **~$142** |

Commercial plans typically pay more than Medicare, so $147 is a conservative anchor. There is no annual maintenance contract.

## Annual revenue by volume

Assumes a full panel at ~$147, a 52 week schedule, and the office already seeing these patients.

| Volume | Panels per year | Gross revenue per year | Net after ~$5 per test |
|---|---|---|---|
| 5 per week (about 1 per day) | 260 | ~$38,200 | ~$36,900 |
| 10 per week (about 2 per day) | 520 | ~$76,400 | ~$73,800 |
| 15 per week (about 3 per day) | 780 | ~$114,700 | ~$110,800 |
| 20 per week (about 4 per day) | 1,040 | ~$152,900 | ~$147,700 |

## When the device pays for itself

The $37,800 device is fully paid off at about **266 full panels**. In time:

| Volume | Time to pay off the device |
|---|---|
| 5 per week | about 12 to 13 months |
| 10 per week | about 6 months |
| 15 per week | about 4 months |
| 20 per week | about 3 months |

After that, the device is yours and roughly $142 of every panel is margin before staff time.

## The takeaway

Even at one full panel a day, the MiniBox+ pays for itself inside the first year and then generates revenue on patients you are already seeing, instead of referring that testing and its reimbursement out the door.

**Check your own New York rates:** CMS Physician Fee Schedule Lookup Tool — https://www.cms.gov/medicare/physician-fee-schedule/search (search CPT codes 94010, 94060, 94726, and 94729 and select your New York locality).

*Figures are illustrative. Actual reimbursement varies by payer mix, plan, and New York locality. Spirometry only visits reimburse less than a full panel.*
