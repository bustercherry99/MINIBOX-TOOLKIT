How a rep reads this in 10 seconds

  • Ratio low → obstruction; FVC low too — but is that restriction or just trapping?
  • Only the TLC answers it: TLC genuinely low → restriction is REAL → mixed.
  • A plain spirometer would guess; the box's lung volumes prove it.
  • This is the strongest case for a full box — spirometry literally cannot make this call.
