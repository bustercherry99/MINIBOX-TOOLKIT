## PART 1 — THE HOOK (open cold, no warmup)

Avi, before I show you anything, I want to ask you one question.

Right now, do you actually know where your reps are, what they are doing, and what is working in the field? Not what they type into a report at the end of the week. What is actually happening, hour by hour, account by account.

*(pause, let him answer, he will say no or "sort of")*

Nobody does. Not because the reps are hiding anything. Because the information does not exist in one place. It is scattered across a dozen tools that do not talk to each other, and half of them we are paying a monthly subscription for.

I have been building the thing that fixes that. And I want to walk you through it, because I think it changes how this entire company runs.

## PART 2 — WHAT YOU ARE LOOKING AT TODAY (hand him the iPad here)

*(Open the toolkit on the iPad and put it in his hands. Let him tap around while you talk.)*

This is the MiniBox Toolkit. It is live, it works, and it is what the reps carry into every office.

Everything a rep needs is in here. The quote builder that mirrors our Salesforce quote. The ROI calculator. The financing tab. The billing helper that tells a practice exactly how they get reimbursed. The forms, the ACH, the RMA, all of it signable on the spot. The training modules. The account map.

*(let him tap, then take the moment)*

Here is the part I want you to hear. This is not the product I am pitching you. This is the front door.

## PART 3 — THE TURN (this is the whole pitch)

Everything a rep touches in that toolkit is data.

Every account they open. Every calculator they run. Every module they click. Every quote they build. Every place they physically stand when they open it.

Right now all of that just disappears. The rep uses the tool, closes it, and the information is gone. We are sitting on the most valuable data this company could have and we are throwing it away every single day.

What I am proposing is simple to say and big to build. We stop throwing it away. We run every rep, every account, every action through one system. One brain. And that brain gets smarter every day the team uses it.

---

## PART 4 — THE NERVOUS SYSTEM (the vision, say it with conviction)

Picture one central system that every part of PulmOne feeds into. The toolkit is just the first thing plugged in. Over time everything plugs in: the reps, the accounts, the orders, the service tickets, the training.

It is a neural network. It learns. And it gives us three things we have never had.

**One. It knows the field.**
The toolkit runs on the reps' phones, so it knows where they go. We will know which offices they are visiting, how often they stop, how long they stay, what territory is getting worked and what territory is getting ignored. Not to babysit them. To find out what actually works. When our best rep closes a deal, the system sees the exact pattern that led to it, the route, the accounts, the sequence, and we can teach that pattern to everyone else.

**Two. It teaches every rep the way that rep learns.**
The system watches what each person clicks and how they move through the material. Some people are visual. Some read. Some need to do it hands on. It figures that out on its own and serves each rep the training that fits how they learn. A new hire ramps in a fraction of the time because the system is teaching them the way their brain actually absorbs it, not a one size fits all binder.

**Three. It tells you where to push.**
Once the data is flowing, you stop guessing. You will see which products are moving in which regions, which reps to put more behind, where to spend the incentive dollars so they actually come back as revenue. You make decisions off what is real, not off a hunch or a lagging report.

None of that is possible without the data. And the only way to get the data is to have the reps living inside one system every day. Which they already are. That is what the toolkit is.

When everything runs through one system, most of that stack becomes redundant. We are not adding another line item. We are collapsing the ones we already pay for into one.

## PART 6 — HOW WE ROLL IT OUT (make it feel safe and phased)

I am not asking you to flip a switch on the whole company tomorrow. This goes in stages, and each stage stands on its own.

**Phase 1. The toolkit, live.** It already exists and the reps already use it. Nothing new to build here. This is the foundation and it is done.

**Phase 2. Turn on the data.** We start capturing what the toolkit already sees. Account activity, usage, location, engagement. This is where the brain starts filling up. Low risk, because the tool does not change for the rep, it just stops throwing the data away.

**Phase 3. The intelligence layer.** Now we act on the data. Adaptive training that molds to each rep. Field patterns that tell us what works. A dashboard for you and the leadership team that shows the whole operation in real time. This is where it starts making the company money instead of just saving it.

**Phase 4. Full integration.** One by one we plug in the rest, and we retire the subscriptions we no longer need. The neural network becomes the single system the whole company runs through.

Every phase delivers something real on its own. If we stopped after Phase 2, you would already know more about your field operation than you do today.
