US Newsletter Program
Annual Plan · 2026–2027 · v1.0

Twelve issues. Approved once. Sent automatically.

One master template, one recognizable masthead, twelve monthly skins. Every issue is written, proofed, and approved ahead of time, loaded into the send platform, and scheduled. The theme is never decoration: each month's hook sells the MiniBox+ through whatever the calendar already has the reader thinking about.

Standing rules (every issue, no exceptions)

Send schedule: first Tuesday of the month, 10:00 AM local recipient time. Same day, same time, all year. Consistency is what makes the masthead an identifier.

What never changes: masthead with the white PulmOne logo chip top left, section order (Hero, Proof, Economics, CTA, What's New, Across the US, Footer), the tagline "PFT Outside of the Box," and one single CTA repeated once.

What changes: the four skin colors, the ribbon stripe, the hero hook, and the monthly content angle. Nothing else.

Quality gate before any issue is loaded: zero em dashes or hyphen punctuation, personalization token present, exactly one CTA, the Across the US proof slot filled with a real verified number or named quote or else deleted, every clinical claim sourced well enough to hand to a physician, and all HTML end tags verified.

Production rhythm: all twelve issues drafted from this plan in one batch. Each issue gets a final 30 minute freshness pass 10 days before its send date (swap in the current proof point, current software version, current promo), then locked and scheduled.
