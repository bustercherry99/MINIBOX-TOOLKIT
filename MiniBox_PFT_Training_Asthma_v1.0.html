How a rep reads this in 10 seconds

  • Low ratio, full lungs → obstruction (air comes out slow, but it all comes out).
  • Numbers jump after the inhaler → reversible → asthma, not fixed COPD.
  • DLCO normal → healthy lung tissue → rules out emphysema.
  • One 90-second MiniBox test captured all three. Spirometry and volumes and diffusion. That a plain spirometer would miss.
